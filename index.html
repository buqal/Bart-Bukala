ABOUT ME

I am enthusiast of IT and new technologies. I deal with graphic design in both 2d and 3d, which is related to my over 15 years of professional experience.

Few words about me

I completed my MSc at Purdue University majoring in Computer Graphics Technology. While studying, I conducted classes in this field for undergraduate students. In the years 2008 - 2020, I taught classes at the Rzeszów University of Technology in the field of information technology. Throughout this period, I developed my skills in using the technology of creating 3D models. I have knowledge in the field of modeling, texturing and lighting scenes using the 3d Studio Max program. I have knowledge of creating projects in Unity. One of these projects was the implementation of an application enabling the use of augmented reality technology when visiting the museum. I was the originator and main developer in "VR/AR labolatory" which operated at the Rzeszów University of Technology in the years 2015 - 2019. During my work at the Rzeszów University of Technology, I was the author of many scientific publications on the use of augmented reality in education. In the form of lectures and practical workshops, I conducted classes from the "Children's University of Technology" series, the aim of which was to familiarize children with 3D modeling and augmented reality technologies. I am currently working as a freelancer implementing projects in the field of comprehensive 3d modeling (modeling, texturing, stage lighting and rendering) in the field of architectural visualization in both 3d Studio Max and Unity.

MY SKILLS

Continuous improvement of skills to my main motto. In my projects, I always try to go beyond the schemes and I always try to learn new technologies, adapting them to the needs of projects that I am creating.

3d Studio Max

The most important part of my professional career. I have extensive knowledge in the field of modeling, texturing, lighting and object rendering.

Photoshop

In 3D design, I use raster graphics in the process creating textures. I also use a Gimp

Illustrator

Some of my projects require uses of raster graphics. The creation of icons, illustrations and texture elements is done by me in Illustrator. I also use Inkscape

Unity

Projects that require user interaction with them are implemented by me in Unity. I use it when importing models from 3ds max. I create lighting, materials and UI elements.

VR / AR

I have been implementing VR projects for a short time. I am still looking for new way to present products to the client. That's I am using VR technology. My earlier projects used augmented reality technologies, but now I put emphasis on VR.

HTML

I started my computer graphic professional career by learning HTML and CSS. I have knowledge of editing websites mainly for simple presentations. In the future, I would like to acquire skills in creating web applications (Java Script).
